About Meteor Madness

A real-time simulator visualizing asteroid impacts and their potential effects on Earth using NASA data

What it does?

Meteor Madness is a high end dahsborad that:


  • Shows asteroids from NASA data.
  • Simulates impact energy and crater size.
  • Displays impact radius on a world map.

Our Goal

To provide an interactive platform that lets users explore near-Earth asteroids and their potential impacts using real NASA data.

It aims to educate and raise awareness about space hazards and planetary safety.
